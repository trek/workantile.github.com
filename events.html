Sponsored Events

Our Clubhouse is maintained on behalf of our Member community. Occasionally we allow non-Members to hold events there.

We offer our space free of charge to groups and events that support our Mission. Your group should be a growing community of peers. You don't have to be interested in Workantile membership, but there should be active mutual interest, promoting and supporting the region as a place for independent work.

Finally, if your event generates revenue through sales or admission charges, we ask that you pass along 10% of the gross you collect to support Workantile Exchange.

Sponsorship

Sponsored events need to arrange for a Contributing Member with at least 3 months of community participation to act as an official Sponsor for every meeting. The Sponsor should

  • ... arrange for the event to be listed on the Workantile Events calendar;
  • ... announce and promote the event to other Workantile Members;
  • ... aid in growing the visitors' community;
  • ... be physically present at the start of each meeting;
  • ... welcome the guests to the Clubhouse, explain our norms and rules, and answer questions.
