The Clubhouse

We share a 3000-square foot private coworking Clubhouse for casual working, heads-down focus time and printing, scanning, day-use lockers and indoor bike parking. We've set aside space for private phone calls, have a kitchenette (stocked with snacks and coffee), bathroom, and conference rooms.

Workantile Exchange is located at 118 S Main, halfway between Huron and Washington Streets on the west side of South Main St. Need a thought-clearing break during the day? Try perusing the cool comics and toys at Vault of Midnight, picking up fresh strawberries at the Ann Arbor farmer's market, finish some quick shopping at the Kerrytown market, grab a book from the Ann Arbor District Library, or nab lunch at any of the great downtown restaurants: it's all within a five minute walk of your Clubhouse.

Becoming a member

  1. Take a tour, and hang out with the community in the Clubhouse for the rest of the day—FREE!

    Call 734.834.2328 or email trek@workantile.com to schedule your tour, or just stop by the Clubhouse between 10-4 any business day and speak to any Member.

  2. Sign up for a Trial week ($35). Use the week to talk with the other Members of the Community, spend time in the Clubhouse, and learn ways to improve your Freelance worklife.

    Recruit at least three Members who will vouch for you when asked.

  3. After the end of the Trial week, apply for Membership and your key.

    You'll need a credit card and a signed License Agreement.
